Shirox is a free, source-available anime library manager for iOS. The app focuses on user control, community modules, and local-first library management.

No Shirox account

Shirox itself does not run a hosted user account system, sell personal data, or operate a tracking profile for users of the app.

Local data

Library entries, playback progress, settings, and module configuration may be stored on your device so the app can provide its core features.

Third-party services

Optional features such as AniList sync, community modules, external media sources, AirPlay, Chromecast, or support links can connect to services outside the Shirox project. Those services may process data under their own privacy terms.
